About Velora AI

Velora is an AI-powered e-commerce recommendation platform that helps customers discover products based on their preferences, budget, and ratings. The system uses Machine Learning algorithms to provide personalized product recommendations and improve the online shopping experience.

🎯 Our Mission

To make online shopping smarter, faster, and more personalized using Artificial Intelligence.

🤖 Technologies Used

Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Logistic Regression, KNN.
